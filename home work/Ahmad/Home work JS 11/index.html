  • Type: Public

American University of Afghanistan (AUAF)
  • Established: 2006
  • Type: Private, Non-Profit
  • Language of Instruction: English
  • Overview: AUAF provides American-style liberal arts education and is known for its programs in Business, Political Science, and Information Technology. It emphasizes critical thinking and leadership skills.
Kardan University
  • Established: 2002
  • Type: Private
  • Enrollment: Over 4,000 students
  • Overview: As the first privately owned university in Afghanistan, Kardan University offers programs in Business Administration, Economics, and International Relations. It is accredited by international bodies such as IACBE and ACBSP.
